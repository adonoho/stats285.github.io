Mark Piercy has been involved with research and software development at Stanford for over 15 years. He is currently a member of the IT team at Stanford Research Computing Center helping Stanford researchers across various desciplines with their high performance computing needs. Mark helps onboard and support new research groups with campus HPC systems. He has previously worked at The Genome Technology Center, HighWire Press, the Genetics Department, Stephen Montgomery’s group and Vijay Pande’s Lab on the Folding@home project. Mark is also an artist and teaches at the Ceramics Studio at Stanford.
